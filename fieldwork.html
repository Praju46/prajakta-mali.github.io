Fieldwork & Data Collection Experience

I have conducted extensive fieldwork as part of academic research and professional projects, involving environmental data collection, hydrological sampling, geomorphological observations, and validation of satellite-derived outputs.

B.Tech Project Fieldwork – Water Quality Assessment

As part of my B.Tech project on water quality analysis of the Lower Koyana Basin, I carried out systematic field surveys focused on in-situ data collection and laboratory-based water quality assessment.

  • Collected surface water samples from 11 different sampling locations along the river stretch.
  • Conducted monthly sampling over a period of three months to capture temporal variations in water quality.
  • Followed standard field and laboratory protocols for analysis of physico-chemical water quality parameters.
  • Integrated field observations and laboratory results with GIS-based spatial analysis for interpretation.

Fieldwork as Junior Research Fellow – Indian Institute of Science (IISc)

During my tenure as a Junior Research Fellow at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru, I participated in multidisciplinary field campaigns supporting river basin management and environmental studies.

  • Collected river sand and sediment samples for geomorphological and textural analysis.
  • Collected surface and subsurface water samples for hydrological and water quality studies.
  • Participated in field-based bioassessment surveys for river health assessment.
  • Conducted floodplain validation surveys to support and verify satellite-derived flood inundation maps.
  • Documented field observations using GPS, photographs, and field notes for geospatial integration.
